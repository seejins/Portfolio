Projects

Get-Meals App
Get-Meals is a full stack application, created so that people who were most affected by COVID-19 are able to find free meals near them. It is a community-driven site, so anyone can add a location and will be added to the database. User's can also search a location and find meals near them that the community has added.
Milal Volunteers
Milal App is a full stack application allowing volunteers to track their own hours. Admin's can also view all their volunteers and the hours they have accumulated. Admin's can also see when those hours were added and the amount added.
Trail Finder
Trail Finder app allows the user the search a location and find any hiking trails nearby to explore. The app implements a Google Maps API to show where the trail is relative to your location. It supplies a short description, the ascent, length, and location using the Hiking Project API.
